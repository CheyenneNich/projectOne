Home Images Stories Newsletter

What?

A various assortment of things that will
hopefully be a speck of sunshine when you need it.
From stories of kind strangers helping those in
need to pictures of silly animals.

Why?

There are so many news and media
outlets constantly showing us all of the
bad things and that can sometimes make a
bad day worse. Reading do-good stories and
looking at cute or funny pictures can help
boost your mood in just a few minutes.
Maybe it can inspire you to spread a little kindness.

Can I contribute?

Yes! If you head over to our newsletter
you can submit positive stores to be shared!
Unfortunatly, we will not accept images for privacy
and legal reasons.
